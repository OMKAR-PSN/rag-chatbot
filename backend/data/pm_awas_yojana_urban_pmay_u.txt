# SCHEME NAME: PM Awas Yojana Urban (PMAY-U)
# ALSO KNOWN AS: PMAY Urban, Housing for All Urban, Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme, CLSS, shehari awas yojana
# KEYWORDS: urban housing loan subsidy, home loan subsidy, EWS LIG MIG housing, CLSS, city house scheme, flat loan interest subsidy, affordable housing
# CATEGORY: Housing
# LAUNCHED BY: Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs
# OFFICIAL WEBSITE: pmaymis.gov.in
# HELPLINE: 1800-11-3377, 1800-11-3388

---

## WHAT IS THIS SCHEME:
PM Awas Yojana Urban helps poor and middle-income families living in cities and towns to buy or build their own house. The government gives a subsidy on home loan interest which reduces the total amount you repay to the bank. For the poorest families, the subsidy can be up to Rs 2.67 lakh (two lakh sixty seven thousand rupees). Some very poor families also get direct money to build a house.

---

## WHO CAN APPLY (ELIGIBILITY):
The scheme has four categories based on income:

EWS (Economically Weaker Section): Annual household income up to Rs 3 lakh
LIG (Lower Income Group): Annual household income Rs 3 lakh to Rs 6 lakh
MIG-I (Middle Income Group): Annual household income Rs 6 lakh to Rs 12 lakh
MIG-II (Middle Income Group): Annual household income Rs 12 lakh to Rs 18 lakh

Additional conditions:
- Applicant or any family member should not own a pucca house anywhere in India
- Should be a resident of urban area (city, town, or municipality area)
- Women ownership is mandatory for EWS and LIG categories — house must be in woman's name
- First-time homebuyers get priority

WHO CANNOT APPLY:
- Persons who already own a pucca house
- Persons whose annual income is above Rs 18 lakh

---

## WHAT WILL I GET (BENEFITS):
- EWS: Interest subsidy of 6.5 percent on loan up to Rs 6 lakh — saves up to Rs 2.67 lakh
- LIG: Interest subsidy of 6.5 percent on loan up to Rs 6 lakh — saves up to Rs 2.67 lakh
- MIG-I: Interest subsidy of 4 percent on loan up to Rs 9 lakh — saves up to Rs 2.35 lakh
- MIG-II: Interest subsidy of 3 percent on loan up to Rs 12 lakh — saves up to Rs 2.30 lakh
- Subsidy is credited directly to your loan account reducing EMI burden

---

## WHAT DOCUMENTS DO I NEED:
- Aadhaar card of all family members
- Income proof — salary slip or income certificate from government
- Address proof showing you live in a city or town
- Proof that you do not own any house — self declaration
- Bank account statements
- Property documents of the house you want to buy or build
- For women applicants — Aadhaar and PAN

---

## HOW TO APPLY (STEP BY STEP):
1. Visit any bank or housing finance company — SBI, LIC Housing, HDFC, or others
2. Apply for a home loan and specifically mention you want PMAY subsidy
3. The bank will verify your eligibility and income
4. Bank submits your details to the Central Nodal Agency (CNA) — either NHB or HUDCO
5. Subsidy is credited to your loan account within 3 to 4 months of loan disbursement
6. Your monthly EMI reduces automatically after subsidy credit
7. You can also check your application status at pmaymis.gov.in
8. Helpline: 1800-11-3377 (toll free)

---

## COMMON QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS:

Q: I live in a city and earn Rs 8 lakh per year. Can I get PMAY subsidy?
A: Yes. You fall in MIG-I category with Rs 6 lakh to Rs 12 lakh income. You get 4 percent interest subsidy on home loan up to Rs 9 lakh. This saves you approximately Rs 2.35 lakh on your total loan repayment.

Q: The house must be in my wife's name. What if she doesn't want a loan?
A: For EWS and LIG categories, the house must be in the name of a woman member of the family. The woman can be co-applicant if the man is the main borrower. The important thing is the woman's name must be on the property documents.

Q: I live in a rented flat in Mumbai. Do I qualify?
A: If you do not own any other house anywhere in India and your income is below Rs 18 lakh per year, you can qualify. Living in a rented house makes you eligible under PMAY Urban.

Q: My bank says I already got PMAY subsidy. Can I apply again?
A: No. PMAY subsidy is given only once per family. If any family member has already availed the subsidy, the family cannot apply again.

Q: How long does it take to get the subsidy?
A: After your home loan is disbursed and your application is submitted by the bank to the nodal agency, the subsidy typically comes in 3 to 6 months and is directly credited to your loan account.

Q: I want to build a house on my own plot in a city. Can I get PMAY?
A: Yes. PMAY Urban covers construction of new house on your own plot as well as purchase of new flat or house. You can take a construction loan and apply for the subsidy.

Q: I applied but did not get subsidy even after 6 months. What do I do?
A: First check your application status at pmaymis.gov.in. Then visit your bank and ask for the status. If still not resolved, call the PMAY helpline at 1800-11-3377.

Q: Can I apply if I currently live in a slum?
A: Yes. Slum dwellers are given special priority under PMAY Urban. Contact your city's Municipal Corporation or Urban Local Body office for special slum rehabilitation assistance.

---

## IMPORTANT WARNINGS:
- PMAY subsidy is given only through registered banks and housing finance companies — not through agents
- Never pay any commission or bribe to get faster processing
- Only purchase or build a house that meets the size criteria for your category
- EWS homes must be maximum 30 square metres carpet area
- LIG homes must be maximum 60 square metres carpet area

---

## PLAIN SUMMARY:
PM Awas Yojana Urban gives interest subsidy on home loans to families in cities and towns who do not own a house. Depending on income, families save Rs 2.30 lakh to Rs 2.67 lakh on their home loan. Apply at any bank or housing finance company with Aadhaar and income documents. Helpline: 1800-11-3377.
