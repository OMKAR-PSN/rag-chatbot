# Scheme Name: PM Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)

## Overview:
PM Ujjwala Yojana gives free LPG gas connections to women from poor families who were previously using firewood or coal for cooking. The scheme aims to reduce health problems caused by indoor smoke pollution and make cooking easier and safer for rural women. The gas connection is given in the name of the woman of the household.

## Eligibility Criteria:
- Women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households
- Women from SECC 2011 listed households
- SC/ST households
- Women who are beneficiaries of PM Awas Yojana, MGNREGA, Antyodaya Anna Yojana, or forest dwellers
- Applicant must be a woman aged 18 years or above
- No LPG connection should already exist in the household
- The connection is issued in the woman's name only

## Key Benefits:
- Free LPG connection with a free first gas cylinder and pressure regulator
- Free installation of gas pipe and stove (in some states)
- First refill cylinder is provided free of charge
- EMI facility to repay the cost of stove and regulator in small monthly instalments deducted from future refill subsidies
- Subsequent LPG refills come with government subsidy credited directly to the bank account

## Documents Required:
- Aadhaar card of the woman applicant
- BPL ration card or SECC data proof
- Bank account details in the woman's name
- Passport size photograph of the woman
- Address proof
- Self-declaration that no LPG connection exists in the household

## How to Apply:
1. Visit your nearest LPG distributor — Indian Oil, HP Gas, or Bharat Gas
2. Ask for the Ujjwala Yojana application form (KYC form)
3. Fill in the form with your personal and family details
4. Submit along with Aadhaar card, BPL card, and bank passbook
5. The distributor will verify your details and process the connection
6. You can also apply online at pmuy.gov.in
7. Once approved the gas connection is delivered to your home free of cost
8. Helpline: 1800-233-3555 (toll-free)
