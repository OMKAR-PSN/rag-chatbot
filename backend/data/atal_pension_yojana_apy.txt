# Scheme Name: Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

## Overview:
Atal Pension Yojana is a pension scheme designed for workers in the unorganised sector such as maids, drivers, farmers, and daily wage workers who do not have a regular pension. If you contribute a small amount every month during your working years, you receive a guaranteed monthly pension of Rs 1000 to Rs 5000 after age 60 for the rest of your life.

## Eligibility Criteria:
- Indian citizen between 18 and 40 years of age
- Must have a savings bank account or post office savings account
- Must have a mobile number linked to the bank account
- Cannot be an income tax payer (since October 2022, income tax payers are not eligible for new enrolments)
- Existing subscribers who were enrolled before October 2022 are not affected

## Key Benefits:
- Guaranteed pension of Rs 1000, 2000, 3000, 4000, or Rs 5000 per month after age 60
- After the subscriber's death, the spouse receives the same pension for their lifetime
- After both subscriber and spouse die, the nominee receives the entire accumulated corpus as a lump sum
- Monthly contribution amount is very small — depends on age at joining and pension amount chosen
- Example: Joining at age 18 for Rs 1000 pension requires only Rs 42 per month contribution

## Documents Required:
- Aadhaar card
- Mobile number linked to the Aadhaar and bank account
- Bank account or post office savings account details
- Nominee's name and relationship details

## How to Apply:
1. Visit any bank branch or post office where you have a savings account
2. Ask for the Atal Pension Yojana enrollment form
3. Fill in your personal details, bank details, preferred pension amount, and nominee details
4. The bank will auto-debit your monthly contribution from your savings account
5. You can also enroll online through your bank's net banking or mobile banking app
6. Search for "APY" in your bank's app — most major banks have this feature
7. Once enrolled, you will receive a PRAN number (Permanent Retirement Account Number) for tracking
