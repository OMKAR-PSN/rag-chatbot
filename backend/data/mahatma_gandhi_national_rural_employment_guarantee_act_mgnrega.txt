# Scheme Name: Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

## Overview:
MGNREGA is a law that guarantees every rural household the right to 100 days of paid work per year. Any adult member of a rural household can demand work from the government and it must be provided within 15 days. If work is not provided within 15 days, the worker is entitled to an unemployment allowance.

## Eligibility Criteria:
- Any adult member (18 years and above) of a rural household can apply
- Applicant must be a resident of a rural area
- Applicant must be willing to do unskilled manual work
- No income, caste, or land ownership criteria — open to all rural adults
- One household can have multiple adult members all working and earning wages

## Key Benefits:
- Guaranteed 100 days of wage employment per household per year
- Wages are paid at state minimum wage rates (varies by state, typically Rs 200 to Rs 350 per day)
- Work is provided within 5 km of the applicant's home
- Wages paid within 15 days of work completion directly to bank or post office account
- If work not provided within 15 days of application, unemployment allowance is paid
- Work includes building roads, ponds, wells, irrigation channels, and tree plantations

## Documents Required:
- Aadhaar card
- Proof of residence (ration card or voter ID)
- Bank account or post office account details
- Passport size photograph
- Job Card (issued free by Gram Panchayat after registration)

## How to Apply:
1. Visit your Gram Panchayat office and ask for MGNREGA registration
2. Submit an application with your household details and Aadhaar card
3. A Job Card will be issued within 15 days — this card lists all earning members
4. When you want work, submit a written or verbal application to the Gram Panchayat
5. Work must be provided within 15 days of your application
6. Wages are credited to your bank account within 15 days of work completion
7. You can check your work history and payments at nrega.nic.in
