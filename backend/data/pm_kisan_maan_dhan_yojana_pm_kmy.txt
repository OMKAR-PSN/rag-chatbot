# SCHEME NAME: PM Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana (PM-KMY)
# ALSO KNOWN AS: Kisan Pension Yojana, PM Kisan Pension, farmer pension scheme, PM-KMY
# KEYWORDS: kisan pension, farmer pension, 3000 rupees pension, 60 saal kisan, kisan maan dhan, agriculture pension
# CATEGORY: Farmer, Pension
# LAUNCHED BY: Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
# OFFICIAL WEBSITE: pmkmy.gov.in
# HELPLINE: 1800-267-6888

---

## WHAT IS THIS SCHEME:
PM Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana gives a monthly pension of Rs 3000 (three thousand rupees) to small and marginal farmers after they turn 60 years old. The farmer pays a small amount every month from age 18 to 40, and after age 60 receives Rs 3000 every month for life. If the farmer dies, the spouse receives half the pension which is Rs 1500 per month.

---

## WHO CAN APPLY (ELIGIBILITY):
- Small and marginal farmers who own 2 hectares or less of farmland
- Age must be between 18 years and 40 years at time of enrollment
- Must be a citizen of India
- Must have a bank account linked to Aadhaar
- Must not be enrolled in any other social security scheme like NPS, ESIC, or EPFO
- Must not be an income tax payer
- Must not be a retired government employee

WHO CANNOT APPLY:
- Farmers who own more than 2 hectares of land
- Persons above 40 years at time of enrollment
- Income tax payers
- Government pensioners

---

## WHAT WILL I GET (BENEFITS):
- Rs 3000 (three thousand rupees) per month after age 60 for life
- If the farmer dies after 60, spouse gets Rs 1500 (fifteen hundred rupees) per month for life
- If the farmer dies before 60, spouse can continue the scheme by paying the remaining contributions
- The government matches the farmer's monthly contribution rupee for rupee
- Monthly contribution is very small — ranges from Rs 55 to Rs 200 depending on age at enrollment

---

## WHAT DOCUMENTS DO I NEED:
- Aadhaar card
- Bank account passbook linked to Aadhaar
- Land ownership documents (Khatian or 7/12 extract showing land is 2 hectares or less)
- Mobile number

---

## HOW TO APPLY (STEP BY STEP):
1. Visit your nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) with your documents
2. The CSC operator will register you on the pmkmy.gov.in portal
3. Your age and contribution amount will be calculated automatically
4. You choose how to pay — auto-debit from bank account monthly or cash at CSC
5. You receive a Kisan Pension Account Number and a card
6. Monthly contributions start immediately after enrollment
7. After age 60, pension of Rs 3000 starts automatically every month
8. Helpline: 1800-267-6888 (toll free)

---

## COMMON QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS:

Q: I am 35 years old. How much do I pay per month?
A: If you enroll at 35 years, you pay Rs 120 per month. The government also adds Rs 120. After age 60 you get Rs 3000 per month for life.

Q: What happens to my money if I die before 60?
A: If you die before 60, your spouse can continue the scheme by paying the same monthly contribution. After the spouse turns 60, they get Rs 1500 per month pension.

Q: I have 3 hectares of land. Can I apply?
A: No. PM Kisan Maan Dhan is only for farmers with 2 hectares or less of land. If you have more than 2 hectares you are not eligible.

Q: Can I stop paying contributions in between?
A: If you stop paying, your account becomes inactive. You can reactivate by paying the missed contributions with interest. It is better to pay regularly without breaks.

Q: I am already enrolled in PM Kisan scheme. Can I also join PM Kisan Maan Dhan?
A: Yes. PM Kisan and PM Kisan Maan Dhan are different schemes. You can be in both. In fact, the government allows PM Kisan instalments to be used to pay PM Kisan Maan Dhan contributions.

Q: I am 42 years old. Can I enroll?
A: No. PM Kisan Maan Dhan enrollment is only for farmers between 18 and 40 years old. If you are above 40, you cannot enroll in this scheme.

Q: Where do I enroll — bank or CSC?
A: Enrollment is done at Common Service Centres (CSC). Banks do not enroll for this scheme. Visit your nearest CSC and carry your Aadhaar, land documents, and bank passbook.

Q: Is this a government guarantee or can the scheme close?
A: This is a central government scheme backed by the Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC). The pension fund is managed by LIC and the Rs 3000 monthly pension is guaranteed by the government.

---

## IMPORTANT WARNINGS:
- Only CSC centres can enroll you — do not pay any private agent to enroll you
- Never pay more than your calculated monthly contribution amount
- Keep your bank account active so auto-debit works every month
- Report fraud at 1930

---

## PLAIN SUMMARY:
PM Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana gives Rs 3000 per month pension to small farmers after age 60. Farmers between 18 and 40 years with 2 hectares or less land can enroll at any Common Service Centre. Monthly contribution is only Rs 55 to Rs 200 and the government adds the same amount. Helpline: 1800-267-6888.
