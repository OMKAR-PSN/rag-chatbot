# SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)
# ALSO KNOWN AS: PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima, Life Insurance Scheme, PMJJBY, jeevan jyoti bima
# KEYWORDS: life insurance, jeevan bima, death cover, Rs 2 lakh life insurance, 436 rupees bima, mrityu bima, term insurance poor
# CATEGORY: Insurance
# LAUNCHED BY: Ministry of Finance
# OFFICIAL WEBSITE: jansuraksha.gov.in
# HELPLINE: 1800-180-1111, 1800-110-001

---

## WHAT IS THIS SCHEME:
PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana gives life insurance of Rs 2 lakh (two lakh rupees) to any Indian adult for only Rs 436 (four hundred thirty six rupees) per year. If a person dies for any reason — accident, illness, or natural causes — their family gets Rs 2 lakh directly in their bank account. This scheme protects poor families from financial crisis after the death of the earning member.

---

## WHO CAN APPLY (ELIGIBILITY):
- Any Indian citizen between 18 years and 50 years of age
- Must have a savings bank account linked to Aadhaar
- Only one PMJJBY policy per person is allowed
- Both men and women can apply
- No medical test is required at the time of enrollment
- Coverage continues until age 55 even though maximum enrollment age is 50

WHO CANNOT APPLY:
- Persons below 18 years or above 50 years of age at time of enrollment
- Persons without a bank account

---

## WHAT WILL I GET (BENEFITS):
- Rs 2 lakh (two lakh rupees) paid to nominee if the insured person dies for any reason
- Covers death due to accident, illness, heart attack, cancer, or any natural cause
- Money is paid directly to the nominee's bank account within 30 days of claim
- Premium of Rs 436 (four hundred thirty six rupees) per year is auto-deducted from bank account
- No medical examination needed

---

## WHAT DOCUMENTS DO I NEED:
- Aadhaar card — mandatory
- Bank account passbook linked to Aadhaar
- Nominee details — name, relationship, and Aadhaar of the nominee
- Mobile number for SMS confirmation

---

## HOW TO APPLY (STEP BY STEP):
1. Visit any bank branch or post office where you have a savings account
2. Ask for PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana enrollment form
3. Fill your personal details and nominee name and relationship
4. Submit the form at the bank counter
5. Rs 436 will be automatically deducted from your account every year in May
6. You will get an SMS confirming your enrollment
7. You can also enroll through your bank's mobile banking or internet banking
8. To make a claim after death, nominee visits bank with death certificate and filled claim form
9. Helpline: 1800-180-1111 (toll free)

---

## COMMON QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS:

Q: My father died due to a heart attack. Can I get the Rs 2 lakh?
A: Yes. PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima covers death from any cause including heart attack. Go to the bank with the death certificate and fill the claim form. Money will come within 30 days. Call 1800-180-1111 for help.

Q: What is the difference between PMSBY and PMJJBY?
A: PMSBY covers only accidental death and costs Rs 20 per year. PMJJBY covers all types of death including illness and costs Rs 436 per year. You should have both for complete protection.

Q: My Rs 436 was not deducted this year. Am I covered?
A: If the premium was not deducted, your policy has lapsed. Visit your bank immediately to renew. You are not covered if premium is not paid.

Q: I am 51 years old. Can I still enroll?
A: You can enroll in PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana only up to age 50. If you are 51 or above, you cannot enroll fresh. But if you enrolled before age 50, you remain covered until age 55.

Q: Can both husband and wife enroll?
A: Yes. Both husband and wife can enroll separately in their own bank accounts. Each gets separate Rs 2 lakh life cover.

Q: I am a daily wage worker with no regular income. Can I apply?
A: Yes. There is no income requirement for PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima. Any person between 18 and 50 years with a bank account can apply.

Q: What documents does my family need to claim after my death?
A: Your family needs: death certificate, filled claim form from bank, nominee's Aadhaar card, and nominee's bank account details. The bank will guide them through the process.

Q: The scheme costs Rs 436. Is there any subsidy?
A: There is no additional subsidy on the Rs 436 premium. But Rs 436 is already a heavily subsidised rate — private life insurance for the same cover costs Rs 3000 to Rs 6000 per year.

---

## IMPORTANT WARNINGS:
- Never pay more than Rs 436 to any agent or officer for this scheme
- Keep your bank account balance at Rs 500 or more every May for auto-debit
- Always register a nominee when enrolling — without a nominee, claim processing is delayed
- Report any fraud at 1930 (Cybercrime helpline)
- Do not sign blank papers given by any insurance agent claiming to help you enroll

---

## PLAIN SUMMARY:
PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana gives Rs 2 lakh life insurance for just Rs 436 per year. It covers death from any cause including illness and accident. Any Indian adult between 18 and 50 with a bank account can enroll at their bank. Helpline: 1800-180-1111.
