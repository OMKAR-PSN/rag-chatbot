# SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)
# ALSO KNOWN AS: PM Suraksha Bima, Accident Insurance Scheme, PMSBY
# KEYWORDS: accident insurance, bima yojana, death insurance, disability cover, 20 rupees bima, suraksha bima, Rs 2 lakh accident, durghatna bima
# CATEGORY: Insurance
# LAUNCHED BY: Ministry of Finance
# OFFICIAL WEBSITE: jansuraksha.gov.in
# HELPLINE: 1800-180-1111, 1800-110-001

---

## WHAT IS THIS SCHEME:
PM Suraksha Bima Yojana gives accident insurance of Rs 2 lakh (two lakh rupees) to any Indian adult for just Rs 20 (twenty rupees) per year. If a person dies or becomes fully disabled in an accident, their family gets Rs 2 lakh. If the person becomes partially disabled, they get Rs 1 lakh. This is the cheapest insurance scheme in India.

---

## WHO CAN APPLY (ELIGIBILITY):
- Any Indian citizen between 18 years and 70 years of age
- Must have a savings bank account or post office savings account
- Account must be linked to Aadhaar
- Only one PMSBY account per person is allowed even if you have multiple bank accounts
- Both men and women can apply
- Farmers, daily wage workers, domestic workers, and all unorganised sector workers are eligible
- People who already have accident insurance from any other source can also apply

WHO CANNOT APPLY:
- Persons below 18 years or above 70 years of age
- Persons without a bank account

---

## WHAT WILL I GET (BENEFITS):
- Rs 2 lakh (two lakh rupees) paid to family if policyholder dies in an accident
- Rs 2 lakh (two lakh rupees) paid if policyholder becomes totally and permanently disabled (loss of both eyes, both hands, both legs, or one eye and one limb)
- Rs 1 lakh (one lakh rupees) paid if policyholder becomes partially disabled (loss of one eye or one limb)
- Money is paid directly to the nominee's bank account
- Premium of only Rs 20 (twenty rupees) per year is deducted automatically from the bank account every June

---

## WHAT DOCUMENTS DO I NEED:
- Aadhaar card — mandatory for identity and linking to bank account
- Bank account passbook — premium is auto-deducted from this account
- Nominee's name and relationship details — person who will receive money in case of death
- Mobile number linked to bank account — for confirmation SMS

---

## HOW TO APPLY (STEP BY STEP):
1. Visit any bank branch or post office where you have a savings account
2. Ask for the PM Suraksha Bima Yojana enrollment form
3. Fill in your name, Aadhaar number, bank account number, and nominee details
4. Submit the form — no documents need to be submitted, only form
5. Rs 20 will be automatically deducted from your account every year in June
6. You will receive an SMS confirmation of enrollment
7. You can also enroll through your bank's mobile banking app by searching PMSBY
8. To make a claim, visit the bank branch with hospital records and accident FIR
9. Helpline: 1800-180-1111 (toll free)

---

## COMMON QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS:

Q: How much does PM Suraksha Bima cost per year?
A: PM Suraksha Bima Yojana costs only Rs 20 (twenty rupees) per year. This amount is automatically deducted from your bank account every June.

Q: My husband died in a road accident. How do I claim the Rs 2 lakh?
A: Visit the bank branch where your husband had the account. Carry the FIR copy, death certificate, and your bank passbook. The bank will give you a claim form. Fill it and submit. Money comes within 30 days. Call 1800-180-1111 for help.

Q: I lost my left hand in a factory accident. Will I get money?
A: Yes. Loss of one limb means partial disability. You will get Rs 1 lakh (one lakh rupees) under PM Suraksha Bima Yojana. Visit your bank with the doctor's disability certificate and accident details.

Q: Can my wife and I both enroll separately?
A: Yes. Both husband and wife can enroll separately in their own bank accounts. Each will get Rs 20 deducted and each will have separate Rs 2 lakh cover.

Q: My Rs 20 was not deducted this year. Am I still covered?
A: If Rs 20 was not deducted, your insurance may have lapsed. Visit your bank immediately and renew the enrollment. You are not covered until the premium is paid.

Q: I don't have Aadhaar. Can I still apply?
A: Aadhaar is required for PM Suraksha Bima Yojana. If you don't have Aadhaar, first get your Aadhaar made at the nearest Aadhaar enrollment centre. Then enroll for the scheme.

Q: Does this scheme cover death due to illness or only accidents?
A: PM Suraksha Bima Yojana covers ONLY accidental death and disability. Death due to illness or natural causes is NOT covered. For illness death, apply for PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana separately.

Q: What if I have two bank accounts? Can I enroll in both?
A: No. You can enroll PM Suraksha Bima Yojana in only one bank account. If you enroll in two accounts, the second premium will be returned and only one policy will be valid.

---

## IMPORTANT WARNINGS:
- No government officer or agent will ask you to pay more than Rs 20 for this scheme
- Do not give your bank details to any stranger claiming to enroll you in PM Suraksha Bima
- Keep your bank account active and maintain at least Rs 20 balance every June for auto-debit
- Report fraud at 1930 (National Cybercrime helpline)
- Always name a nominee when enrolling — without a nominee, claim process becomes complicated

---

## PLAIN SUMMARY:
PM Suraksha Bima Yojana gives Rs 2 lakh accident insurance for just Rs 20 per year. Any Indian adult with a bank account can join by filling a simple form at their bank. If an accident causes death or disability, the money goes directly to the family's bank account. Helpline: 1800-180-1111.
