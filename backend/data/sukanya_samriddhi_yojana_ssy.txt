# Scheme Name: Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)

## Overview:
Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana is a savings scheme created specifically for the future of girl children. Parents or guardians open a savings account in their daughter's name and deposit money regularly. The government gives a high interest rate on the savings and the money is used for the girl's education or marriage when she grows up.

## Eligibility Criteria:
- Only for girl children who are 10 years old or younger
- The account must be opened by the parent or legal guardian
- Only one account per girl child is allowed
- Maximum two accounts per family are allowed (one for each daughter)
- For families with twins or triplets as the second birth, more accounts may be allowed
- The girl must be an Indian citizen

## Key Benefits:
- Current interest rate is 8.2 percent per year (revised every quarter by the government)
- Interest earned is completely tax-free under Section 80C
- Minimum deposit is Rs 250 per year and maximum is Rs 1.5 lakh per year
- Account matures when the girl turns 21 years old
- Partial withdrawal of 50 percent is allowed after the girl turns 18 for education
- Tax deduction benefit for the depositor under Section 80C up to Rs 1.5 lakh per year

## Documents Required:
- Birth certificate of the girl child
- Aadhaar card of parent or guardian
- Identity proof of parent (Aadhaar, PAN, voter ID, or passport)
- Address proof of parent
- Photograph of parent and girl child
- Initial deposit amount (minimum Rs 250)

## How to Apply:
1. Visit any post office or authorised bank branch such as SBI, Bank of Baroda, PNB, Canara Bank, or most private banks
2. Ask for the Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana account opening form
3. Fill in the form with the girl's details and parent's details
4. Submit the form with all documents and the initial deposit
5. A passbook will be issued in the girl child's name
6. Make deposits regularly — minimum once a year to keep the account active
7. You can also link the account for online deposits through your bank's internet banking
