# SCHEME NAME: Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP)
# ALSO KNOWN AS: BBBP, Save Girl Child Scheme, daughter scheme, beti ki padhai, girl education scheme
# KEYWORDS: beti bachao, girl child scheme, daughter education, sex ratio scheme, girl school dropout, female foeticide, Sukanya account girl
# CATEGORY: Women and Child
# LAUNCHED BY: Ministry of Women and Child Development
# OFFICIAL WEBSITE: wcd.nic.in/bbbp-schemes
# HELPLINE: 181 (Women Helpline), 1098 (Child Helpline)

---

## WHAT IS THIS SCHEME:
Beti Bachao Beti Padhao is a government programme that works to protect the lives of girl children, ensure they are educated, and give them equal opportunities as boys. The scheme focuses on districts where the number of girls per 1000 boys is very low. It provides direct financial help through Sukanya Samriddhi accounts and also runs awareness campaigns to stop sex-selective abortion and ensure girls stay in school.

---

## WHO CAN APPLY (ELIGIBILITY):
- All families with girl children in India can benefit from this scheme
- Special focus on families in districts with low sex ratio
- Girls who are at risk of dropping out of school
- The main financial benefit is through Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana which is open to girls up to age 10

---

## WHAT WILL I GET (BENEFITS):
- Awareness and support to keep girl children in school until Class 12
- Financial support through Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (8.2 percent interest savings account for girl)
- Some states give cash incentives for birth of girl child and for keeping girl in school
- Community support programmes against female foeticide and child marriage
- Medical and legal support through district offices for families facing gender discrimination

---

## WHAT DOCUMENTS DO I NEED:
- For Sukanya Samriddhi account — birth certificate of girl, Aadhaar of parent, deposit amount
- For state-level cash benefits — varies by state (generally Aadhaar, birth certificate, school enrollment proof)

---

## HOW TO APPLY (STEP BY STEP):
1. For Sukanya Samriddhi account — visit any post office or bank branch with the girl's birth certificate and parent's Aadhaar
2. Open a Sukanya Samriddhi account in the girl's name with minimum Rs 250 deposit
3. Deposit Rs 250 to Rs 1.5 lakh per year for up to 15 years
4. Money grows at 8.2 percent interest per year tax-free
5. Account matures when girl turns 21 and money is used for education or marriage
6. For other BBBP benefits — contact your district Women and Child Development (WCD) officer
7. To report female foeticide, child marriage, or girl child abuse — call 181 (Women Helpline)

---

## COMMON QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS:

Q: My daughter is 8 years old. Can I still open a Sukanya account?
A: Yes. Sukanya Samriddhi account can be opened for girls up to age 10. Visit any post office or bank with her birth certificate and your Aadhaar.

Q: I have two daughters. Can I open Sukanya accounts for both?
A: Yes. You can open one Sukanya Samriddhi account for each daughter. Maximum two accounts per family. Each account gets 8.2 percent interest per year.

Q: Is there direct cash given under Beti Bachao Beti Padhao?
A: The central scheme itself does not give direct cash. But many states have their own schemes under BBBP that give cash at girl's birth, at school admission, and at completion of Class 10 and 12. Check your state government's WCD website.

Q: My neighbour is planning to abort because they found out it is a girl. What can I do?
A: Call 181 (Women Helpline) immediately. Sex-selective abortion is illegal in India under the PCPNDT Act. You can report anonymously.

Q: My daughter has dropped out of school due to family pressure. How do I get help?
A: Contact the district Women and Child Development office or call 181. They can intervene to help the girl get back into school and support the family.

---

## IMPORTANT WARNINGS:
- Sex-selective abortion is illegal — anyone found doing this faces imprisonment
- Child marriage before age 18 for girls is illegal — report at 181 or 1098
- No one should ask for dowry at any marriage — report at 181

---

## PLAIN SUMMARY:
Beti Bachao Beti Padhao works to protect girl children and ensure their education. Families can open Sukanya Samriddhi accounts for daughters with just Rs 250 at any post office or bank for 8.2 percent tax-free interest. For any problem related to girl child safety or education, call Women Helpline 181 or Child Helpline 1098.
