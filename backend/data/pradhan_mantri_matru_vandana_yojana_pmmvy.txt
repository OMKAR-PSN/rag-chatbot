# SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)
# ALSO KNOWN AS: Maternity Benefit Scheme, PMMVY, matru vandana, pregnant women scheme, Rs 5000 pregnant, delivery scheme
# KEYWORDS: pregnant woman scheme, maternity money, delivery Rs 5000, first child scheme, garbhavati mahila yojana, matru vandana, anganwadi pregnant
# CATEGORY: Women and Child
# LAUNCHED BY: Ministry of Women and Child Development
# OFFICIAL WEBSITE: pmmvy.wcd.gov.in
# HELPLINE: 1800-115-534

---

## WHAT IS THIS SCHEME:
PM Matru Vandana Yojana gives Rs 5,000 (five thousand rupees) to a woman when she is pregnant with her first living child. The money is given in three instalments to help the pregnant woman rest properly, eat nutritious food, and take care of herself. The scheme covers all women who are pregnant for the first time whether they are in a village or a city.

---

## WHO CAN APPLY (ELIGIBILITY):
- Women pregnant with their first living child
- All women including those in rural and urban areas
- Women from all castes and income groups can apply
- Minimum age of 19 years at time of pregnancy registration
- Government employees and women already covered under any paid maternity benefit scheme are not eligible

WHO CANNOT APPLY:
- Women pregnant with second or subsequent children (only first child is covered)
- Women who are central or state government employees
- Women already receiving maternity benefits under any other law

---

## WHAT WILL I GET (BENEFITS):
- First instalment Rs 1,000 after early registration of pregnancy at Anganwadi or health centre
- Second instalment Rs 2,000 after receiving at least one antenatal check-up
- Third instalment Rs 2,000 after the child is born and first cycle of vaccination is completed
- Total benefit Rs 5,000 (five thousand rupees) per woman
- Money is transferred directly to the woman's bank account

---

## WHAT DOCUMENTS DO I NEED:
- Aadhaar card of the pregnant woman (mandatory)
- MCP card (Mother and Child Protection card) issued by Anganwadi
- Bank account in the woman's name linked to Aadhaar
- Mobile number of the woman or husband

---

## HOW TO APPLY (STEP BY STEP):
1. As soon as you know you are pregnant, visit your nearest Anganwadi centre or government health sub-centre
2. Get yourself registered as a pregnant woman and receive a MCP card (Mother and Child Protection card)
3. The Anganwadi worker or ASHA worker will help you fill Form 1A for PMMVY registration
4. Submit the form with Aadhaar and bank account details
5. First Rs 1,000 comes to your bank account within 30 days of registration
6. Go for your antenatal check-up and get it recorded in the MCP card
7. Submit Form 1B after antenatal check-up — second instalment of Rs 2,000 is released
8. After the baby is born and vaccinations are given, submit Form 1C — third instalment Rs 2,000 comes
9. Helpline: 1800-115-534 (toll free)

---

## COMMON QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS:

Q: I am pregnant with my second child. Can I get Rs 5000?
A: PM Matru Vandana Yojana gives the Rs 5000 benefit only for the first living child. For second and subsequent children, you do not get PMMVY benefit.

Q: I delivered at a private hospital. Am I still eligible?
A: Yes. PMMVY is not linked to where you deliver. It is linked to pregnancy registration at Anganwadi and vaccination of the baby. Register at Anganwadi and follow the steps regardless of hospital type.

Q: I did not register during pregnancy. My baby is already 3 months old. Can I still apply?
A: Yes but apply immediately. There is a time limit — you must apply within 730 days of the baby's date of birth. Visit your Anganwadi with MCP card, baby's vaccination records, and Aadhaar.

Q: The Anganwadi worker says my name is not registered. What do I do?
A: Visit the Child Development Project Officer (CDPO) at your block level. Carry your Aadhaar, pregnancy records, and MCP card. File a written complaint if registration is refused.

Q: My baby died soon after birth. Do I still get the money?
A: If the child was born alive but died after birth, you are still eligible for the instalments already due. If the child was stillborn, the third instalment linked to vaccination will not apply.

Q: Can my husband apply on my behalf?
A: The registration must be in the pregnant woman's name and the money comes to the woman's bank account. The husband can help fill forms and submit but the account must be in the wife's name.

---

## IMPORTANT WARNINGS:
- Register for PMMVY as early as possible in pregnancy — do not wait
- Make sure your bank account is in your own name (woman's name) not husband's name
- The Anganwadi worker should not charge any fee to register you
- Report any issues at 1800-115-534

---

## PLAIN SUMMARY:
PM Matru Vandana Yojana gives Rs 5000 to women pregnant with their first child in three instalments. Every pregnant woman should register at her nearest Anganwadi centre as soon as possible to start getting the money. Registration is free and the money comes directly to the woman's bank account. Helpline: 1800-115-534.
