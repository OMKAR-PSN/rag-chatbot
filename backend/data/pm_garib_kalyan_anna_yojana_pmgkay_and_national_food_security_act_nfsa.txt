## WHAT IS THIS SCHEME:
The National Food Security Act guarantees subsidised or free food grains to 81 crore poor people in India. Every eligible person gets 5 kg of rice, wheat, or coarse grain per month. Under PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana, this food is given completely FREE of cost to all National Food Security Act beneficiaries. The grain is distributed through ration shops (Fair Price Shops) in every village and town across India.

---

## WHO CAN APPLY (ELIGIBILITY):
There are two types of ration cards:

Priority Household (PHH): For families above absolute poverty but still poor
- Decided by state government survey and census
- Each member gets 5 kg grain per month free

Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY): For the absolute poorest families
- Landless labour, marginal farmers, daily wage earners, rural artisans
- Elderly, widows, disabled persons living alone
- Each family (regardless of size) gets 35 kg grain per month free

---

## WHAT WILL I GET (BENEFITS):
- 5 kg of rice, wheat, or coarse grains per person per month completely FREE
- AAY families get 35 kg per household per month completely FREE
- Grain is available at your local ration shop (Fair Price Shop) every month
- PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana extended this to be free — no payment required

---

## WHAT DOCUMENTS DO I NEED:
- To get a ration card — Aadhaar card of all family members, proof of residence, income certificate
- To collect ration every month — ration card or Aadhaar-based biometric verification at ration shop
- Mobile number linked to Aadhaar for OTP verification at some ration shops

---

## HOW TO APPLY (STEP BY STEP):
1. For a new ration card — visit your Tehsil office or Food and Civil Supplies office
2. Fill the ration card application form with details of all family members
3. Submit with Aadhaar, residence proof, and income certificate for each family member
4. Officer will verify and issue ration card within 30 to 45 days
5. Once you have a ration card, visit your local Fair Price Shop (ration shop) every month
6. Give your ration card and verify with fingerprint or OTP on Aadhaar
7. Collect your entitled grain for the month
8. In many states you can check your ration entitlement online on the state food department website
9. Helpline: 1967 or 14445

---

## COMMON QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS:

Q: I moved to a new city. How do I transfer my ration card?
A: Under the One Nation One Ration Card scheme, you can collect ration from ANY ration shop in India using your existing ration card and Aadhaar. You do not need to transfer the card. Just go to the nearest ration shop in your new city.

Q: My name was removed from the ration card after marriage. What do I do?
A: After marriage, a woman can be added to her husband's ration card. Visit the Tehsil food office with marriage certificate and Aadhaar. You can also get a separate ration card for your new household.

Q: The ration shop owner is not giving me full grain. What can I do?
A: Call the PDS helpline 1967 immediately and report the shortage. You can also complain at the District Food Supply Officer office. Always check the weight on a scale at the ration shop.

Q: I don't have a ration card. Can I still get grain?
A: Without a ration card you cannot get ration. Apply immediately at your Tehsil office. Until you get a card, ask if your local Gram Panchayat has any emergency food scheme.

Q: The ration shop says grain is not available this month. What do I do?
A: This is not allowed. The ration shop must have grain every month. Report to the District Food Officer or call 1967. You are entitled to receive the previous month's shortfall in the next month.

Q: My Aadhaar fingerprint is not working at the ration shop machine. What do I do?
A: Ask the ration shop owner to use OTP-based authentication instead of fingerprint. An OTP will come to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. If still not working, contact the district food office.

Q: I have a ration card but my name is not on it. Can I still get grain?
A: You cannot collect grain for a name that is not on the ration card. Visit the Tehsil food office to get your name added. This takes 2 to 4 weeks.

Q: How do I know how much grain I am supposed to get?
A: Check the label outside your ration shop — it must display entitlements. You can also check online at your state food department website or call 1967.

---

## STATE-SPECIFIC NOTES:
- Some states like Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, and Telangana give additional items like oil, sugar, and pulses through PDS
- Delhi gives subsidised flour (atta) through PDS in addition to grain
- Many states have their own state-specific ration card schemes in addition to the central NFSA scheme
- Check your state food department website for additional state benefits

---

## IMPORTANT WARNINGS:
- Never pay the ration shop owner more than the official price (which is currently Rs 0 for PMGKAY beneficiaries)
- Report short weight, poor quality grain, or demands for money at 1967
- Do not sell or transfer ration to others — this is illegal and can result in ration card cancellation
- Ration card is a valuable document — keep it safely and report loss immediately to Tehsil office

---

## PLAIN SUMMARY:
National Food Security Act and PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana together give 5 kg of free grain per person per month to 81 crore poor Indians. Every eligible person must have a ration card from their Tehsil office to collect grain from the nearest ration shop. Under One Nation One Ration Card, you can collect grain from any ration shop in India. Helpline: 1967.
