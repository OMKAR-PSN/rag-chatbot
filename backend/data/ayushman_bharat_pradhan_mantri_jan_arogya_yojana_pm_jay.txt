# Scheme Name: Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

## Overview:
Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY is the world's largest government-funded health insurance scheme. It gives poor and vulnerable families free health insurance of up to Rs 5 lakh per year. The money can be used for treatment at any government or empanelled private hospital in India.

## Eligibility Criteria:
- Families listed in the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 data are automatically eligible
- Rural families who live in kutcha houses, have no adult earning member, belong to SC/ST, or earn income mainly from manual labour
- Urban workers including rag pickers, domestic workers, street vendors, construction workers, sanitation workers, home-based workers, and beggars
- There is no upper limit on family size — the entire family is covered
- There is no age limit — newborns to elderly all covered
- You do not need to pay any premium or registration fee

## Key Benefits:
- Free health cover of Rs 5 lakh per family per year
- Covers hospitalisation costs including room charges, doctor fees, medicines, tests, and surgery
- Covers pre-existing diseases from day one — no waiting period
- Covers 1,949 medical and surgical packages across 27 specialties
- Cashless and paperless treatment at any empanelled hospital
- Covers 3 days before hospitalisation and 15 days after discharge expenses
- Free Ayushman card issued to every eligible family member

## Documents Required:
- Aadhaar card (mandatory for identity verification)
- Ration card to verify family details
- Mobile number linked to Aadhaar
- Caste certificate if applicable (for SC/ST families)
- Income certificate if needed

## How to Apply:
1. Visit pmjay.gov.in and click "Am I Eligible" to check if your family is in the list
2. Enter your mobile number and verify with OTP
3. If eligible, visit the nearest government hospital or Common Service Centre (CSC)
4. Carry your Aadhaar card and ration card
5. The hospital Ayushman Mitra will verify your details and issue your Ayushman card on the spot
6. Use this card at any empanelled hospital for free treatment
