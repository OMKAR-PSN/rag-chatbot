# SCHEME NAME: PM Mudra Yojana (PMMY)
# ALSO KNOWN AS: Mudra Loan, PMMY, Shishu loan, Kishore loan, Tarun loan, small business loan, self employment loan
# KEYWORDS: mudra loan, Rs 50000 loan, Rs 5 lakh loan, Rs 10 lakh loan, small business loan, self employment loan, shishu kishore tarun, no collateral business loan
# CATEGORY: Employment, Livelihood
# LAUNCHED BY: Ministry of Finance
# OFFICIAL WEBSITE: mudra.org.in
# HELPLINE: 1800-180-1111

---

## WHAT IS THIS SCHEME:
PM Mudra Yojana gives loans from Rs 50,000 (fifty thousand) to Rs 10 lakh (ten lakh rupees) to small business owners, shopkeepers, vendors, artisans, and self-employed persons. No collateral or guarantee is needed for these loans. The scheme has three categories — Shishu for loans up to Rs 50,000, Kishore for Rs 50,000 to Rs 5 lakh, and Tarun for Rs 5 lakh to Rs 10 lakh.

---

## WHO CAN APPLY (ELIGIBILITY):
- Any Indian citizen who wants to start or expand a small business
- Non-corporate and non-farm businesses — manufacturing, trading, services
- Street vendors, shopkeepers, artisans, tailors, mechanics, repair shops, food vendors
- Women entrepreneurs are given special preference and lower interest rates
- SC/ST entrepreneurs are given special preference
- No income limit — the loan amount is determined by the business need

WHO CANNOT APPLY:
- Agricultural farm activities (for farm loans apply for KCC)
- Persons who have defaulted on previous bank loans and have not resolved the issue

---

## WHAT WILL I GET (BENEFITS):
- Shishu: Loans up to Rs 50,000 (fifty thousand rupees)
- Kishore: Loans from Rs 50,000 to Rs 5 lakh (five lakh rupees)
- Tarun: Loans from Rs 5 lakh to Rs 10 lakh (ten lakh rupees)
- No collateral required for loans — no need to mortgage property
- A Mudra card (debit card) is given to withdraw loan money as needed
- Repayment period is 3 to 5 years depending on the loan

---

## WHAT DOCUMENTS DO I NEED:
- Aadhaar card
- PAN card (mandatory for loans above Rs 50,000)
- Proof of business — shop registration, GST certificate, or any proof of business activity
- Passport size photographs
- Bank account statements for last 6 months
- Business plan or description of how the loan will be used
- For Kishore and Tarun — balance sheet or income tax returns may be needed

---

## HOW TO APPLY (STEP BY STEP):
1. Visit any government bank branch — SBI, Bank of Baroda, Canara Bank, PNB — or microfinance institution
2. Tell the bank you want a Mudra loan and mention the category — Shishu, Kishore, or Tarun
3. Fill the Mudra loan application form
4. Submit with Aadhaar, PAN, business proof, and bank statements
5. Bank officer will assess your business and repayment capacity
6. For Shishu loans, approval usually comes within 7 to 15 days
7. Loan amount is credited to your bank account or a Mudra card is issued
8. You can also apply online at udyamimitra.in
9. Helpline: 1800-180-1111

---

## COMMON QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS:

Q: I want to start a small tailoring business. Can I get Mudra loan?
A: Yes. Tailoring is exactly the type of business Mudra supports. Apply for a Shishu loan of up to Rs 50,000 for sewing machine and other equipment. Visit your nearest government bank with Aadhaar and business plan.

Q: Does Mudra loan need any collateral or guarantor?
A: No collateral is required for Mudra loans. You do not need to mortgage your house or land. You also do not need a guarantor.

Q: What is the interest rate for Mudra loans?
A: Interest rates vary by bank — typically 8 to 12 percent per year. Women entrepreneurs and SC/ST borrowers usually get lower rates. Compare rates at two or three banks before applying.

Q: I already have a small shop. Can I get a Mudra loan to expand it?
A: Yes. Mudra loans are given both to start new businesses and to expand existing ones. Tell the bank you want to expand and show your existing business documents.

Q: My Mudra loan application was rejected. What do I do?
A: Ask the bank for the reason in writing. Common reasons are poor credit history or insufficient business proof. Fix the issue and reapply. You can also try a different bank or a microfinance institution.

Q: Is there a government subsidy on Mudra loans?
A: There is no direct interest subsidy on Mudra loans. But there is no collateral requirement which itself is a big benefit. Women SC/ST entrepreneurs may get slightly lower rates.

Q: How many Mudra loans can I take?
A: You can start with a Shishu loan and upgrade to Kishore and then Tarun after good repayment history. Each upgrade gives you a larger amount.

Q: I am a farmer but also do a small business. Can I get Mudra loan?
A: Yes. If your small business activity is non-agricultural (shop, service, trade) you can get Mudra loan for that activity. For your farm needs, apply for KCC separately.

---

## IMPORTANT WARNINGS:
- Apply for Mudra loans only at registered banks — not through agents who promise faster approval for a fee
- Never pay commission to anyone for Mudra loan processing
- Repay on time to build credit score for future larger loans
- If any bank refuses a Mudra loan without valid reason, complain to the Banking Ombudsman

---

## PLAIN SUMMARY:
PM Mudra Yojana gives small business loans from Rs 50,000 to Rs 10 lakh without any collateral or guarantor. Any person who wants to start or expand a small non-farm business can apply at any government bank branch with Aadhaar and business details. Helpline: 1800-180-1111.
