# Scheme Name: PM Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

## Overview:
PM Fasal Bima Yojana is a crop insurance scheme that protects farmers from financial loss when their crops are damaged due to natural disasters like floods, drought, hailstorm, pest attacks, or unseasonal rain. Farmers pay a very small premium and the government pays a large portion of the insurance cost. If the crop is damaged, the farmer receives compensation directly in their bank account.

## Eligibility Criteria:
- All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas are eligible
- Both loanee farmers (who have taken crop loans) and non-loanee farmers can apply
- Sharecroppers and tenant farmers are also eligible
- Loanee farmers are enrolled automatically by their bank — they can opt out if they wish
- Non-loanee farmers must apply voluntarily before the cutoff date

## Key Benefits:
- Insurance cover for total loss or partial loss of crop
- Premium rates are very low — 2 percent for Kharif crops, 1.5 percent for Rabi crops, 5 percent for commercial and horticultural crops
- Government pays the remaining premium amount
- Compensation paid for losses due to drought, flood, hailstorm, cyclone, pest and disease
- Post-harvest losses covered for up to 14 days after harvest
- Losses due to fire, lightning, storm, hailstorm covered even before harvest

## Documents Required:
- Aadhaar card
- Bank account passbook linked to Aadhaar
- Land ownership documents or lease agreement
- Sowing certificate from Patwari or Gram Panchayat
- Mobile number

## How to Apply:
1. Loanee farmers are registered automatically by their bank when they take crop loans
2. Non-loanee farmers must visit their nearest bank branch or CSC before the enrollment deadline
3. Enrollment deadline is typically last week of July for Kharif and last week of December for Rabi
4. Carry your Aadhaar, bank passbook, and land or sowing documents
5. You can also apply online at pmfby.gov.in or through the Farmer app
6. After applying, an insurance certificate is issued
7. In case of crop damage, inform your bank or insurance company within 72 hours
8. Helpline: 1800-200-7710
